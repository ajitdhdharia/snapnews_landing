Snapnews Customer Data Privacy Policy  
  
At Snapnews.ai, we prioritize your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Customer Data Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use the Snapnews mobile app and related services.

**Personal Information:**  
- **Account Information:** When you create an account, we collect information such as your name, email address, and password.  
- **Profile Information:** You may provide additional information, such as a profile picture and preferences.  
  
**Usage Data:**  
- **App Usage:** We collect information about how you use the app, including the news articles you read, the time spent on the app, and your interaction with features.  
- **Device Information:** We collect data about the device you use to access Snapnews, such as your IP address, device type, operating system, and browser type.  
  
**Location Data:**  
- We may collect and process information about your location if you grant us permission to do so. This helps us provide localized content and improve our services.  
  
## 2. **How We Use Your Information**  
  
**To Provide and Improve Services:**  
- Personalize your experience by showing content tailored to your interests.  
- Improve the functionality and performance of the Snapnews app.  
- Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.  
  
**To Communicate with You:**  
- Send you updates, newsletters, and promotional materials (if you opt-in).  
- Notify you about changes to our services, terms, or policies.  
  
**For Security and Compliance:**  
- Monitor and analyze usage patterns to detect and prevent fraud or abuse.  
- Ensure compliance with legal obligations and protect the rights and safety of Snapnews and our users.  
  
## 3. **Sharing Your Information**  
  
We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:  
  
**With Service Providers:**  
- We may share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as hosting, data analysis, and customer support. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.  
  
**For Legal Reasons:**  
- We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).

**Access and Update:**  
- You can access and update your personal information through your account settings.  
  
**Opt-Out:**

**Delete Account:**  
- You can delete your Snapnews account by contacting us at admin@snapnews.ai. Upon deletion, we will remove your personal information, except where retention is required by law.

We implement a variety of security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.
